Frequently Asked Questions

Check out our Frequently Asked Question below

1. Do you take all of the photos yourself?

Yes! All the photos you see on my website, as well as on my social media accounts, have all be taken, edited and published by HEAD Photography.

2. Can I buy any of your photos directly from the website?

No, we currently only offer photography services and do not sell individual photos directly, whether on the website or not. This is a service and feature we are looking to add inthe future so keep your eyes open!

3. How much is it for your photography services?

It depends on a number of circumstances, so your best bet is to contact us and we can discuss your personal needs in more detail, after which we will ahve a clearer idea of the costs.

4. Do you provide videography services?

No, we do not currently provide any videography services.

5. Are you available for weekend work?

Yes, we are avialble on weekends. Book in early as we usually have a large number of weeding shoots booked during the weekend.

6. How many hours do you suggest we set aside for wedding day photos?

It depends a lot on what kind of photos you want on the day and how many we will need to take. Please get in touch so we can discuss on more depth.

7. What happens if we go over the contracted amount of time?

We understand that not everything goes exactly as planned on the day. We will never leave or pack up before or at your contracted time is up. We will ask if you would like to extend, and charge you accordingly.

8. When can we expect to see our photos from our engagement session?

Post-production for wedding photos usually takes approximately 4-5 weeks after the photoshoot.
