Portfolio

About

Reuben Head is a New Zealand landscape, portrait, candid and event photographer, based in Wellington, New Zealand - although travelling frequently throughout the country. He is open to work anywhere around the country, wherever you may be!

Reuben is a keen traveller, and most of his photography is captured during his moments journeying the globe, from Asia to the Middle East to the United States.

When Reuben isn't snapping photos, you'll see him baking in the kitchen, trying all sorts of food around the city, and watching as much football as he can. He also has recently picked up web development which he is looking to explore further in the furutre.
